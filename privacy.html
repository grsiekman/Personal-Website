If you engage with the dark mode switch, the site will use local storage to store your preference and your first switch click on your device. This allows your theme preference to persist every time you return to the site. The switch click data is stored to prevent the site from serving the privacy pop up after you've already seen it once. As this is local storage, I don't/can't collect any information from this. If you wish to remove this after interacting with the switch, click here. By default, the website attempts to set theme based on your system theme preferences.
